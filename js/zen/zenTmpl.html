CALM DOWN AND REALIZE YOU ARE NOT IMPORTANT
Exercise Action Description
Vipassana Breathing Watch your breath with awareness
Metta Bhavana Emotion Become aware of yourself, and focus on feelings of peace, calm, and tranquillity.
Mindfulness of Breathing Breathing After the out-breath you count one, then you breathe in and out and count two, and so on up to ten, and then you start again at one.
Mantra Chanting Sit with spine erect, and eyes closed. Repeat the mantra in your mind, silently, over and over again during the whole session.
